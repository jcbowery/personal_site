About Me

I am a passionate and detail-oriented SDET (Software Development Engineer in Test) and DevOps professional, driven by a commitment to quality and efficiency in software development and delivery. With a strong foundation in test automation and infrastructure-as-code practices, I excel at building scalable testing frameworks and streamlining CI/CD pipelines. My approach ensures that quality assurance is seamlessly integrated into every phase of the software development lifecycle, enabling teams to deliver reliable, high-performing solutions faster.

My experience spans a wide range of tools and technologies, including Selenium, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. I specialize in designing test automation strategies, implementing continuous testing in DevOps workflows, and optimizing deployment processes. Whether it's automating regression tests, integrating performance monitoring, or managing complex infrastructure, I thrive on tackling technical challenges and finding innovative solutions to support team goals.

Beyond the technical aspects, I believe in fostering collaboration between development, QA, and operations teams. By promoting a culture of shared responsibility and continuous learning, I aim to bridge gaps and encourage innovation. With a keen eye for detail and a focus on scalability, I bring a holistic perspective to DevOps and testing that empowers organizations to build, test, and deploy software with confidence.

Philosophy

I believe in products first. Customers care about having a quality product and as professionals we should care that we do everything we can to deliverit. That means thorough testing, taking our time before release, and creating the best guard rails we can to prevent issues before they reach our customers eyes.
